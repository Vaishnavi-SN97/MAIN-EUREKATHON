# Quick Start Guide

## üöÄ Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox recommended)
- Webcam access
- Good lighting for hand detection

### Installation

No installation needed! The app runs in your browser.

### First Time Setup

1. **Allow Camera Access**
   - Click "Allow" when prompted for camera permission
   - Make sure your webcam is working and properly lit

2. **Start the Game**
   - Click "START GAME" on the landing screen
   - Listen to the character's introduction

3. **Select Your First Level**
   - Click on "Level 1 - Counting" (only unlocked level)
   - Other levels are locked until you earn enough points

### Playing the Game

#### Gesture-Based Levels (Counting, Addition, Subtraction)

1. **Position Yourself**
   - Sit 2-3 feet from your webcam
   - Ensure your hand is fully visible
   - Good lighting is essential

2. **Show Fingers**
   - Hold up your hand with fingers extended
   - The system counts extended fingers
   - Answer displays in real-time

3. **Answer Questions**
   - "Show me 3 fingers" ‚Üí Hold up 3 fingers
   - "2 + 1 = ?" ‚Üí Hold up 3 fingers
   - Wait for the green checkmark!

#### Drawing-Based Level (Shapes)

1. **Raise Your Hand**
   - Extend your index finger
   - Keep other fingers closed

2. **Draw in Air**
   - Move your index finger to draw
   - Draw smoothly and continuously
   - Complete the shape by connecting the end to start

3. **Shape Tips**
   - **Circle**: Draw a smooth, round shape
   - **Triangle**: Three distinct corners
   - **Square**: Four corners, roughly equal sides

### Troubleshooting

#### Hand Not Detected
- Check camera permissions
- Improve lighting
- Move closer to camera
- Ensure hand is fully in frame
- Try reloading the page

#### Fingers Not Counting Correctly
- Extend fingers fully
- Keep hand steady
- Don't overlap fingers
- Try different hand angles

#### Drawing Not Working
- Raise only index finger
- Keep hand open (thumb away from fingers)
- Draw larger shapes
- Complete the shape (connect end to start)

#### Sound Not Playing
- Check browser volume
- Unmute the tab
- Check system audio settings
- Refresh the page

### Tips for Best Experience

1. **Environment**
   - Sit in well-lit area
   - Plain background works best
   - Avoid backlighting

2. **Hand Position**
   - Keep hand 1-2 feet from camera
   - Face palm toward camera
   - Spread fingers clearly

3. **Drawing**
   - Draw slow and steady
   - Make shapes large
   - Close the shape completely

4. **Progression**
   - Complete all tasks in a level
   - Earn points to unlock next level
   - Don't skip levels
